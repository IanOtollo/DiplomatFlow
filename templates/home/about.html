About IT Helpdesk System

Centralized IT support and asset management for the Ministry.

The IT Helpdesk System streamlines equipment tracking, issue resolution, and resource assignment across all directorates. It provides a secure platform for managing technical assets and support requests.

Key capabilities
  • Task creation, assignment, and tracking
  • ICT equipment and directorate assignment
  • Reports and analytics
  • User and role management

Built with Django and designed for reliability and ease of use.
