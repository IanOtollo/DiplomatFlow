About MOFA Task Tracker

Diplomatic workflow management for the Ministry of Foreign Affairs.

MOFA Task Tracker streamlines task coordination, equipment assignment, and reporting across directorates. It provides a secure, centralized platform for managing tasks, ICT equipment, and team performance.

Key capabilities
  • Task creation, assignment, and tracking
  • ICT equipment and directorate assignment
  • Reports and analytics
  • User and role management

Built with Django and designed for reliability and ease of use.
